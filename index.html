Nutty

Nutty cheeses are fairly straightforward in the sense that the flavor is reminiscent of nuts. Common nutty flavors in cheese include walnuts and hazelnuts.

Piquant

Piquant is similar to tangy, but refers to the way a "sharp," aged acidic cheese makes your mouth feel.

Buttery

"Buttery" cheeses tend to be rich and creamy, with a high butterfat content and a subtle sweetness.
